Grade Ranges

Course grades are assigned using the following ranges:

  • A = 93 or above
  • A- = 90 to 92.99
  • B+ = 87 to 89.99
  • B = 83 to 86.99
  • B- = 80 to 82.99
  • C+ = 77 to 79.99
  • C = 73 to 76.99
  • C- = 70 to 72.99
  • D = 60 to 69.99
  • F = 59.99 or below

Grades will not be rounded up, for example, a 89.99 is a B+ and will not be changed to a 90.
